Jane Etish-Andrews

Director

Jane Etish-Andrews is the Director of the Tufts University International Center since 1983. She began her career at Boston University, as an Advisor to ESL (English as a Second Language) students, and later become the Assistant Director of the International Student & Scholar Office at Boston University. n her role as Director of the Tufts University International Center, she manages a team providing immigration and student services to 700 undergraduate and graduate students and to 250 faculty and research scholars. In addition to her immigration expertise, she is also well-versed in Student Affairs. Jane is the Co-Chair of the Social Justice Initiative at Tufts University where she works with domestic and international students on issues of identity and cultural competency. Jane is an active member of NAFSA: The Association of International Educators, where she has been a leader both on the regional and national level. She is an adjunct faculty member at Lesley University's graduate program in Intercultural Relations. Jane has a Bachelor's degree in French Language and Literature and a Master's degree in Teaching English as a Second Language, both from Boston University.

Patrick Himes

International Student and Scholar Advisor

Patrick joined the International Center in August of 2014. Pat moved to Boston from his home state of Pennsylvania in 2013 and earned a Master's degree in Intercultural Relations at Lesley University. From 2008-2010, Patrick served in the U.S. Peace Corps, where he taught English in a small village in the Kyrgyz Republic, and gained an immense appreciation for other languages and cultures. Whitney Sullivan

International Student and Scholar Advisor

Whitney joined the International Center in September 2014. She holds an M.A. in Intercultural Relations from Lesley University and a B.A. in Political Science, International Relations, and Hispanic Studies from East Carolina University. Whitney has lived abroad in Saalfelden, Austria and Montevideo, Uruguay as an exchange student, and has spent much time traveling in Western Europe and South America.
